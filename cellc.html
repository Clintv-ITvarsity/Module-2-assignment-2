ABOUT CELL C

Cell C opened its doors in 2001, since providing South Africans with exceptional mobile service prices. Despite a large market influx of mobile service providers preceding Cell C’s establishment, the service provider has excelled in the market.

With stores found nationwide, Cell C offers mobile services to customers in Cape Town, Johannesburg, Durban, East London, Port Elizabeth, Bloemfontein, and many more. Stores are also easily accessible as they are commonly found in shopping centres around the country. The services offered by Cell C include data deals, phone deals, laptop deals, Wi-Fi deals, iPhone deals, tablets, contracts, and upgrades. Contracts are available for customers wishing to purchase any of the abovementioned products. These products are available at well-priced packages for over an agreed upon period of time. Customers enjoy the Cell C mobile experience as their Wi-Fi deals are offered at affordable prices compared to the company’s competitors.

Cell C additionally runs promotions and specials on a regular basis, ensuring that customers receive the best deals on the market. Find information on these deals via the Cell C website https://www.cellc.co.za/cellc/home, where you can also access a catalogue and brochure of up-and-coming packages at fantastic rates.
