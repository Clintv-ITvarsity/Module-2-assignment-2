ABOUT mr price

Mr Price Group Limited, is a publicly traded retail company based in South Africa. Founded in 1985, Mr Price opened in 1987 after the owners bought a controlling interest in John Orrs Holdings, whose trading divisions at the time were The Hub and Miladys. Their primary trading divisions are as follows: Mr Price (primarily apparel) Mr Price Sport – Formed in 2006. Mr Price Home Miladys – Acquired in 1987 Sheet Street – Acquired in 1996 Mr Price Money – Started 2007. Mr Price Cellular – Started 2015. Power Fashion – Acquired in 2020. YuppieChef – Acquired in 2021.
